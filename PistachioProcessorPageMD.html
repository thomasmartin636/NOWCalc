Pistachio Mating Disruption Research Summary

Created by: Contact Thomas Martin for more information: (559) 321-4488 tmartin@trece.comThomas Martin

Mating Disruption is estimated to reduce damage in Pistachios by 50% or more. Typically, as you accumulate larger, contiguous acreage under mating disruption, you can expect better results. Therefore, the model will estimate a 50% reduction in damage as the benefit of Mating Disruption. For example:

  • 10% Damage -> 5% Damage with MD
  • 5% Damage -> 2.5% with MD
  • 2% Damage -> 1% with MD

The next step is to choose your Processor to determine which Quality Bonus Schedule is used. Most processors incentivize lower damage by adding between $0-$0.25/lbs on Edible Open Inshell Yield (~80% of total Yield) based on the damage assessment and other factors. Processors also penalize EXTREMELY HIGH damage. If you process your own nuts, choose "No Processor" and quality bonus incetives will not be used, but you'll still see the benefit of Yield preservation. If you don't want to use any of these QB schedules, "Averge Model for Quality Bonuses" takes the average bonus across the processors included.
