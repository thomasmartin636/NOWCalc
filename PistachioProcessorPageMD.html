Pistachio Mating Disruption Research Summary

Created by: Contact Thomas Martin for more information: (559) 321-4488 tmartin@trece.comThomas Martin

Mating Disruption has been shown to reduce damage due to NOW by 50% or more. Typically, as you accumulate larger, contiguous acreage under mating disruption, you can expect better results.

The model estimates a 50% reduction in damage by decreasing the Original Rejects % by 50%. For example:

  • 10% Damage -> 5% Damage
  • 5% Damage -> 2.5%
  • 2% Damage -> 1%
