From 2003-2006, Joel Siegel and Brad Higbee recorded the relatioship between mummies/tree and NOW damage in almonds for almost 2,000 almond blocks (Higbee 2009).

Most processors incentivize lower damage by adding between $0-$0.20/lbs based on the damage assessment and several other factors. Most processors also penalize EXTREMELY HIGH damage, as well.
