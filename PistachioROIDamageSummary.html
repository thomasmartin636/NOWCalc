The 5 pillars of IPM for NOW in Pistachios are:

  • Winter Sanitation
  • Mating Disruption
  • Monitoring
  • Timely Harvest
  • Chemical Application

The model uses academic research of each practice to INDIVIDUALLY estimate an expected reduction in damage. Then, it combines the estimate with information from processors to estimate the dollar value of the damage reduction.
