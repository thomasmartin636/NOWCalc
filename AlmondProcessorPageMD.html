Almond Mating Disruption Research Summary

Created by: Contact Thomas Martin for more information: (559) 321-4488 tmartin@trece.comThomas Martin

Mating Disruption has been shown to reduce damage due to NOW by 50%-80% (Haviland 2020). Typically, as you accumulate larger, contiguous acreage under mating disruption, you can expect better results. In general, when mating disruption is applied on acreage of 40 acres or more, damage due to NOW is reduced by 50%. Therefore, the model will estimate a 50% reduction in damage as the benefit of Mating Disruption. For example:

  • 10% Damage -> 5% Damage
  • 5% Damage -> 2.5%
  • 2% Damage -> 1%

The next step is to choose your Processor to determine which Quality Bonus Schedule is used. Most processors incentivize lower damage by adding between $0-$0.20/lbs based on the damage assessment and several other factors. Most processors also penalize EXTREMELY HIGH damage, as well. If you process your own nuts, choose "No Processor" and quality bonus incetives will not be used. If you don't want to use any of these QB schedules, "Averge Model for Quality Bonuses" takes the average bonus across the processors included.
